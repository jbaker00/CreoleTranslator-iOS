# Creole Translator

A free iOS app for translating between **Haitian Creole and English** using AI-powered speech recognition and translation.

- [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy)
- [Terms of Use](terms-of-use)

## About

Creole Translator lets you speak in Haitian Creole or English and instantly get an AI-powered translation in the other language. It's designed to help bridge communication for the Haitian Creole–speaking community.

### Features

- **Speech-to-text** — Speak Haitian Creole or English and see your words transcribed in real time
- **AI Translation** — Powered by Groq AI for fast, accurate translations
- **Text-to-speech** — Hear the translation read aloud using natural-sounding voices
- **Bidirectional** — Switch between Creole → English and English → Creole with one tap
- **Translation history** — Review your past translations in-app
